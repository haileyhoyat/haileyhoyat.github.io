Hello, thank you for checking out our 2022 Data Days map.

This is a crowdsourced map for attendees' notable Cleveland places. These places can be a place with a fond memory, a place you get lost, or a favorite place you frequent.

Put a pin down, and check out the notable places from other Data Days attendees as well.

Pins on the map are labeled as the following

F = a favorite place
M = a memorable place
L = a place that's easy to get lost at
